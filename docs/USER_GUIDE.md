# User Guide - Instagram Stories Webhook v0.21.1

## 👋 What is this project?

The **Instagram Stories Webhook** is a powerful tool designed to help you automate and manage your Instagram Story content. Instead of posting manually from your phone, you can submit content, set a schedule, and let the system handle "handless" posting to your Instagram account.

## 🌟 Key Features

- **Easy Submissions**: Upload images or videos directly through the dashboard.
- **Smart Scheduling**: Choose exactly when you want your content to go live.
- **Admin Control**: Review content before it gets published to ensure quality.
- **Auto-Sync**: Connects directly to your Facebook/Instagram account securely.
- **Reliability**: Automatic retries if Instagram is temporarily down.

---

## 🛠️ How it works (The 4 Steps)

1.  **Connect Your Account**: Link your Instagram Business account via Facebook login.
2.  **Submit Content**: Upload your story (meme, photo, or video) and add a caption.
3.  **Review & Approve**: An admin checks the content to make sure it looks great.
4.  **Sit Back & Relax**: The system automatically publishes the content at the scheduled time.

---

## 👥 Who can do what?

- **User**: Can submit content and see their own history.
- **Admin**: Can approve or reject submissions from any user and manage the schedule.
- **Developer**: Can manage system settings, check technical logs, and fix issues.

---

## 📱 Monitoring on the go

You don't need to be at your computer to know if everything is working:
- **iPhone Widget**: You can add a widget to your iPhone home screen that shows if the "autopilot" (Cron) is running correctly.
- **Notifications**: The system keeps track of any issues and logs them for the technical team to review.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What happens if my video is the wrong size?**
A: The system automatically adjusts videos to fit the Instagram Story format (9:16 ratio) so they look perfect.

**Q: Can I delete a post after it's scheduled?**
A: Yes, you can cancel any pending post from the "Schedule" dashboard before its scheduled time.

**Q: Is my account safe?**
A: Yes! We use secure, official Meta (Facebook) logins and never store your password.
